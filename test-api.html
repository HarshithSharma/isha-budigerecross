⚠️ Common Issues:
  • CORS Error: Your Apps Script must be deployed with "Who has access: Anyone" (not "Anyone with Google account")
  • 404 Error: Check that the URL in config.js is correct and ends with /exec
  • 401/403 Error: The script needs to be authorized and deployed with "Execute as: Me"
  • Empty Response: Your Google Sheet tabs might not exist or have no data
